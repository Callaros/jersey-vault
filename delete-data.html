Data deletion

Delete your data

Jersey Vault does not create proprietary user accounts and does not operate backend servers for user-created collection data.

How to delete data associated with the App

Users may delete their information at any time directly from their own device.

  1. Delete individual jerseys, collections or wishlist items inside Jersey Vault.
  2. Clear the App data from the device settings to remove locally stored data.
  3. Uninstall the App to remove the local app data from the device.
  4. If you used Google Drive backup, delete the backup from your own Google Drive AppData storage.

Because the developer does not maintain proprietary user accounts or backend copies of user-created collection data, deletion requests do not require contacting the developer.

Data that can be deleted

Deletion may include jersey catalog data, collections, statistics, wishlist items, app preferences, attached photos, processed images and custom notes stored locally or in optional user-created Google Drive backups.
