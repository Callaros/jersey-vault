Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed while using Jersey Vault, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Jersey Vault ("the App").

Jersey Vault is designed to help users organize, catalog and manage their sports jersey collections while respecting their privacy.

The App has been designed following a privacy-first approach. Most information remains stored locally on the user's device and is never transmitted to the developer.

2. Data Controller

Developer: Luca Antonelli

Country: Italy

lucaantonelli1999@gmail.com

3. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android and iOS versions of Jersey Vault.

4. Information We Process

The App does not require users to create an account.

The App does not maintain proprietary user accounts.

Most information entered into the App is stored exclusively on the user's device.

The App may process:

  • jersey information;
  • team names;
  • player names;
  • seasons;
  • jersey type;
  • authenticity;
  • condition;
  • estimated value;
  • wishlist information;
  • collection names;
  • front and back photos;
  • custom notes;
  • application settings.

This information is created entirely by the user.

The developer cannot access this information.

5. Local Storage

Jersey Vault stores data locally using an on-device database.

Local data includes:

  • jersey catalog;
  • collections;
  • statistics;
  • wishlist;
  • application preferences;
  • processed images.

This information never leaves the device unless the user explicitly performs a backup.

6. Photos and Camera

The App may request permission to access the photo library and the camera.

These permissions are used solely to allow users to attach photos to jerseys. Photos remain stored locally and are never uploaded to the developer.

7. Background Removal

The App includes an automatic background removal feature. Image processing is performed locally on the user's device. Images are not uploaded to external servers for this feature.

8. Text Recognition

The App includes Optical Character Recognition (OCR) using Google ML Kit. OCR processing is performed locally on the device. Images processed for text recognition are not uploaded to the developer.

9. Google Drive Backup (Optional)

The App offers an optional backup and restore feature using Google Drive.

If enabled:

  • users authenticate using their own Google account;
  • backups are stored only in the application's private AppData folder;
  • the App cannot access other files stored in Google Drive;
  • backups are created only when initiated by the user.

The App requests only the permissions required for backup and restore functionality.

10. Google Sign-In

Google Sign-In is used exclusively to authenticate the user for Google Drive backup and restore.

The App does not use Google Sign-In for marketing, profiling, analytics or social features.

The developer does not maintain a copy of the user's Google account information.

11. Advertising

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may process certain information including, depending on device configuration and user consent:

  • Advertising ID;
  • device identifiers;
  • IP address;
  • diagnostic information;
  • performance information;
  • approximate location;
  • ad interaction data.

Such processing is performed by Google according to Google's own Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy

If required by applicable law, users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom or Switzerland will be presented with a consent message before personalized advertising is shown.

12. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob);
  • Google Sign-In;
  • Google Drive API;
  • Google ML Kit.

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy and terms.

13. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell personal data.

The developer does not rent personal data.

The developer does not share user-created collection data with third parties.

Certain information may be processed by third-party providers solely to provide their services, such as:

  • Google AdMob;
  • Google Authentication;
  • Google Drive.

14. Data Retention

Local information remains stored until:

  • deleted by the user;
  • application data is cleared;
  • the application is uninstalled.

Google Drive backups remain stored until deleted by the user.

The developer does not retain copies of user backups.

15. Data Deletion

Users may delete their information at any time by:

  • deleting jerseys inside the App;
  • clearing application data;
  • uninstalling the App;
  • deleting backups stored in their own Google Drive.

Because the developer does not operate backend servers for Jersey Vault, deletion requests do not require contacting the developer.

16. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, processing is based on:

  • Article 6(1)(a) - user consent;
  • Article 6(1)(b) - performance of the requested service;
  • Article 6(1)(f) - legitimate interest in providing and securing application functionality.

17. International Transfers

Some third-party providers used by the App, including Google, may process information outside the user's country of residence. Such providers implement appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable privacy legislation.

18. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are adopted to protect user information. However, no electronic storage method or transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

19. Children's Privacy

Jersey Vault is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

20. User Rights

Depending on applicable legislation, users may have the right to:

  • access their personal data;
  • correct inaccurate information;
  • erase personal data;
  • restrict processing;
  • object to processing;
  • request data portability;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.

Since Jersey Vault stores user-created information locally, most of these rights can be exercised directly by the user without contacting the developer.

21. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

The updated version becomes effective immediately upon publication.

Users are encouraged to review this page regularly.
